Stabilize your Capital (EtherCash)

Looking for a Currency, not a financial asset? So, take some EtherCash!
EtherScale is a financial asset, while EtherCash is currency that pairs with your Country Currency instantly & free of fees.

Smart Contract

EtherCash is a SmartContract at EtherScale Blockchain made to be a DAILY CURRENCY which pairs with any Fiat Money using ISO 4217 currency code.

Pairing Stability

Each EtherCash abstraction is a stable token pairing to a State Currency, like:
1 EtherUSD = 1 USD (US Dolar);
1 EtherEUR = 1 EUR (Euro),
and so on...

Inflation Protection

Each ISO Code (USD, EUR, BRL...) has it own limited supply;
Issues or burns are based on State Currencies pairing values;
Like: 1 USD = 0.81 EUR, so there are more ETHUSD than ETHEUR.

ZBS tokens and economy of EtherScale

EtherScale uses ZBS tokens in its economy. ZBS tokens can be used in different cases:

Pay for usage of EtherScale services

Payment for promotions

Having big listing it’s always hard to be noticed naturally. Especially if you are unknown project.

ZBS tokens can be used to pay for promotions of crowdsale campaigns and bounties. Having hundreds to thousands of campaigns on site it will be hard to outstand. By paying for, let’s say 1000 tokens will give a campaign 1000 extra views by showing the campaign on the top of EtherScale site.

The similar situation could be with bounties or job entries: there could be thousands of different bounties and to get on top of other bounties, a project will pay some ZBS tokens.

Try demo

Reward for bounty

You can pay for bounty with tokens of your project that is going to launch a crowdsale. This is a good way for project to save money and to get some work done cheaper. But from the other side this way of payment is not safe for workers/contributors, because there is a big chance that a project will not success in crowdsale because of a big competition with other projects.

ZBS tokens as a payment reward for bounty could be a good alternative. Every project could have ZBS tokens on its deposit (for example after a required topup during publishing of ICO campaign), and these tokens could be used as a reward for bounty tasks instead of unissued tokens of project. ZBS tokens will be more respected and safe way to pay for the job, such as they will be tradeable at that moment in contrast to unissued tokens of a project.
